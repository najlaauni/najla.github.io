Festival of Ideas (FOI) 2025 is an annual event that celebrates innovation, creativity, and collaboration among universities, industries and communities. It takes place at the Putrajaya International Convention Centre (PICC). The event allowed local and international participants to participate in forums, exhibitions, workshops, and showcases. FOI aims at developing a culture where innovative thinking and sharing of knowledge are the main attributes thus making the youth and students the main players to turning an idea into reality and contributing to Malaysia's development.
